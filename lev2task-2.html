Biography

Bill Gates, born William Henry Gates III on October 28, 1955, is an American business magnate, software developer, and philanthropist. He co-founded Microsoft Corporation in 1975 with Paul Allen, which revolutionized the computer software industry and made Gates one of the wealthiest individuals in the world. Gates led the company as CEO and chairman until stepping down in 2000 to focus on his philanthropic endeavors. Through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, he has donated billions to various global health, education, and poverty alleviation initiatives. Gates' visionary leadership in technology and commitment to philanthropy have made a profound impact on the world, inspiring future generations to innovate and give back.
